  • Week 1: Understanding Self

    Objective: Provide tools and techniques to address and heal from past trauma, fostering resilience and emotional well-being.
  • Week 3: Business Development
    Objective: Equip participants with essential business skills and knowledge to develop and grow their business ideas.
